## who I am

I study **Computer Engineering** and **Finance & Banking** at the same time — not as a hedge, but because the problems I care about live where both meet.

Markets move in microseconds. Hardware can keep up. Math explains why.  
I'm here for that triangle.

| lane | what I'm chasing |
|:-----|:-----------------|
| **quantitative finance** | pricing intuition, risk, microstructure, systems that respect latency |
| **FPGA / digital design** | parallel pipelines, HDL, moving logic closer to the wire |
| **applied mathematics** | models that survive contact with reality |
