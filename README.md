## About

I am pursuing **Computer Engineering** and **Finance & Banking** concurrently. My academic focus sits at the intersection of financial markets, computational systems, and mathematical modeling — areas that increasingly depend on one another in quantitative and low-latency applications.

| Focus area | Direction |
